Privacy Policy (POPI Compliant)

Effective Date: January 3, 2025

This policy outlines how Monkae (referred to as "we," "us," or "our") handles your Personal Information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: For identifying the booking.
  • Email: **Crucial for service communication, specifically via Email.**
  • Service & Time: For fulfilling the contractual service.

2. Purpose of Processing

Your Personal Information is collected and processed strictly for the following purposes:

  • To **process and manage** your service appointment booking.
  • To **communicate confirmation, reminders, and service-related messages** exclusively via the WhatsApp number provided.
  • For internal **reporting and service history** (kept securely in our database).

3. Consent (POPIA Principle)

By checking the consent box on the booking form, you explicitly agree that we may process your Name and Phone Number for the purpose of booking management and WhatsApp communication as described above. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us directly.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your Personal Information to any third parties. Your data is stored securely using **Google Cloud Firestore** and is accessible only to authorized MIDA administrators for the purpose of managing bookings.

5. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your Personal Information against unauthorized access, loss, or damage. All administrative access is protected by a secure login protocol.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights under POPIA, please contact us:

Email: info@monkae.co.za

Thank you for trusting MIDA with your Personal Information.
